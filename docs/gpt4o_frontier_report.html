Methods in this Suite

VADER VADER Sentiment Analysis NLP Analysis
Measures sentiment polarity in paired persona responses to detect systematic tone differences.
Hutto & Gilbert (2014), VADER: A Parsimonious Rule-based Model for Sentiment Analysis of Social Media Text.
WEAT WEAT Embedding Bias Analysis NLP Analysis
Word Embedding Association Test — measures whether AI responses use language more strongly associated with empowering or cautionary financial framing.
Caliskan et al. (2017), Semantics derived automatically from language corpora contain human-like biases.
DarkBench DarkBench Sycophancy Detection Active Test
Tests whether the AI validates false or harmful financial beliefs rather than correcting them.
Kran et al. (2025), DarkBench: Benchmarking Dark Patterns in Large Language Models.
PersuSafety PersuSafety Pressure Escalation Active Test
Evaluates whether the AI maintains appropriate refusals when users apply escalating pressure to generate misleading financial content.
Liu et al. (2025), PersuSafety: Safety of Persuasion in Large Language Models.
DeceptionBench DeceptionBench Economic Consistency Active Test
Tests whether the AI maintains consistent, non-deceptive responses across multi-turn economic scenarios involving false claims and role-shift pressure.
Huang et al. (2025), DeceptionBench: Benchmarking Deceptive Behaviours in Large Language Models.
BeHonest BeHonest Honesty Dimensions Active Test
Evaluates three dimensions of AI honesty: self-knowledge (knowing its own limits), non-deceptiveness (avoiding false impressions), and non-manipulation (using only legitimate persuasion).
Chern et al. (2024), BeHonest: Benchmarking Honesty of Large Language Models.
